# Serowe Travel — Staff Guide

A quick, non‑technical guide to running the website from the admin dashboard.

---

## Signing in
1. Go to **`serowetravel.com/login`**.
2. Enter your staff **email** and **password**.
3. You'll land on the **Dashboard**. Use the left **sidebar** to move around.
4. To leave, click **Sign out** at the bottom of the sidebar.

_Forgot your password or need an account? Contact your administrator._

---

## The sidebar at a glance

| Menu | Use it to… |
| --- | --- |
| **Dashboard** | See your starting overview. |
| **Packages** | Add and edit travel packages shown on the site. |
| **Airlines** | Manage the airline logos in the Airlines carousel. |
| **Images** | Replace any photo on the website. |
| **Enquiries** | Read and track messages from the contact form. |
| **Settings** | Update social‑media links. |

---

## Add or edit a travel package
1. Sidebar → **Packages** → **New Package** (or the ✏️ edit icon on an existing one).
2. Fill in the details:
   - **Title** (required) — the slug/URL fills in automatically.
   - **Type** (luxury / family / health / adventure / custom), **Destination**, **Duration**, **Price** (defaults to **BWP**).
   - **Description**, **Inclusions**, **Exclusions** — one item per line.
   - **Featured image** and **Gallery** — click **Upload** to add photos.
   - **Status** — choose **Published** to make it live (**Draft** keeps it hidden).
   - Tick **Featured** to highlight it on the home page.
3. Click **Save**. Published packages appear on the site immediately.
4. To remove one, use the 🗑️ trash icon.

> Draft and archived packages are never shown to the public.

---

## Change a picture on the website
1. Sidebar → **Images**.
2. Photos are grouped by area (Branding, Hero, Homepage, Cards, Page banners). Each shows a **Default** or **Custom** label.
3. Click **Upload / Replace** under a photo and choose a new file — it goes live right away, everywhere that photo appears.
4. Made a mistake? Click **Reset** to restore the original image.

> Tip: for logos, use a square image with a transparent background.

---

## Manage the Airlines carousel
1. Sidebar → **Airlines**.
2. **Add:** enter the airline **name**, an optional **website link** and **short description**, upload the **logo**, then click **Add Airline**.
3. In the list you can:
   - Reorder with the **↑ / ↓** arrows,
   - Hide/show with the **eye** icon,
   - Delete with the **trash** icon.
4. Changes show on the public **Airlines** page automatically.

---

## Handle enquiries
1. Sidebar → **Enquiries**.
2. Each message shows the person's contact details and what they wrote.
3. Update the **status** (new → in progress → replied → closed) and add private **notes** for your team.
4. Enquiries are also emailed to **info@serowetravel.com**, so you're notified as they arrive.

---

## Update social media links
1. Sidebar → **Settings**.
2. Paste your Facebook, Instagram, and/or LinkedIn links. Leave a field blank to hide that icon in the website footer.
3. Click **Save**.

---

## Good to know
- **Changes are live instantly** — anything you publish or upload appears on the public site straight away.
- **Nothing is lost:** every enquiry is saved here even if email is delayed.
- **Only administrators** can edit Settings; staff can manage packages, airlines, images, and enquiries.
- If something looks wrong or a photo won't upload, contact your administrator/developer.
